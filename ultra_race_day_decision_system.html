04 Communication Scripts — Critical
Rule

You are not arguing. You are executing pre-agreed rules.

When You Must Override Her
"I love you. We agreed on this rule."
"This is a safety call, not a performance call."
"We're stopping to protect you."
When She Wants To Quit (But Safe)
"You don't need to feel good. You just need to move."
"Let's walk 5 minutes and reassess."
"Next step only. Not the finish."
When She's Spiraling
"You're okay. This is normal."
"Just forward. Nothing else matters."
When She Misses Cutoff
"You showed up. You did the work."
"We'll learn from this. Not now."
