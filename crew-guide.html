02 What You Do At Each Stop — Script Mode
START (0 mi) — Prevent Stupid Mistakes
"Eat early. Walk the sand. Don't be a hero."
  • Final gear check
  • Confirm she has bottles (NO CUPS race)
  • Leave immediately after start
MILLSTON (~5 mi) — Danger: She Feels Amazing → Lies To You

Ask ONLY:

"How do your legs feel?"
"Any hot spots?"
  • Top off fluids
  • Take unnecessary gear (headlamp, layers)
Do Not
Make changes. Let her stand around.
SHALE ROAD — FIRST PASS (~16 mi) — First Real Job
  • Calories?
  • Hydration?
  • Feet?
  • Refill EVERYTHING
  • Push calories into her
"Next job = survive Wildcat loop."
SHALE ROAD — SECOND PASS (~20 mi) — Truth Checkpoint

Look for:

  • Slower movement
  • Quiet answers
  • Bad posture

If struggling → Fix ONE thing only:

  • Food
  • Feet
  • Fluids
"Just get to Wildcat. Nothing else matters."
WILDCAT (~24 mi) — CRITICAL POINT — Where Races Live Or Die
Cutoff = 2:45 PM
  • Is she cold?
  • Is she eating?
  • Is she mentally present?
  • Add warm layer if needed
  • Give real food
  • Caffeine if she uses it
"You're finishing this. Just keep moving."
Do Not
Talk about time goals. Overwhelm her.
FINISH (~31.5 mi)
  • Warmth
  • Food
  • Sit her down before she tips over

04 Gear Setup — Mobile Repair Station
Core System: 4 Bin Method

Label bins: Bin 1 = Millston, Bin 2 = Shale, Bin 3 = Wildcat, Bin 4 = Finish

Each bin = pre-loaded for that stop.

What Goes In Every Bin
CategoryItems
1. FUELGels / chews she already uses, PB&J halves, pretzels / chips, banana, electrolyte mix (pre-portioned)
2. HYDRATION1–2 full bottles ready to swap, extra soft flasks (pre-filled if possible)
3. FEET + CHAFESpare socks (2–3 pairs), body glide / anti-chafe stick, small towel, blister kit (tape, bandages, needle)
4. CLOTHINGDry shirt, light jacket or wind shell, gloves + hat, buff
5. QUICK MEDIbuprofen (if she uses it), salt tabs, ginger chews (for nausea)
F-H-F-C-M — Fuel • Hydration • Feet • Clothing • Meds
Extra — Keep In Car, Not Every Bin
  • Folding chair
  • Blanket or sleeping bag
  • Thermos (hot drink = HUGE at Wildcat)
  • Power bank + cables
  • Printed race guide + maps
